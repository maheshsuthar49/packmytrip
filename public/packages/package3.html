Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Srinagar - Houseboat Stay

Arrive at Srinagar Airport, where our representative will greet you. Transfer to a luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake. Enjoy a Shikara ride in the evening while witnessing the sunset over the lake. Stroll through the local market before an overnight stay in the houseboat.

Day2

Srinagar - Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, explore the famous Mughal Gardens - Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Visit the Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic views of Srinagar. In the evening, shop for authentic Kashmiri handicrafts, Pashmina shawls, and dry fruits. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day3

Srinagar - Gulmarg

After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg, known for its lush green meadows and skiing slopes. Experience the thrilling Gondola ride (Asia's highest cable car) up to Apharwat Peak. Enjoy activities like horse riding, skiing (in winter), and snow biking before an overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day4

Gulmarg - Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam, also called the Valley of Shepherds. En route, visit the Saffron fields of Pampore and the Awantipura Ruins. On arrival, relax at Lidder River or explore Aru Valley and Betaab Valley for mesmerizing landscapes. Stay overnight in Pahalgam.

Day5

Pahalgam - Sightseeing & Leisure

Spend the day at leisure, exploring Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland of Kashmir) on a pony ride or visiting Chandanwari, the starting point of the Amarnath Yatra. Enjoy local delicacies before returning for an overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day6

Pahalgam - Sonmarg - Srinagar

After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg, the “Meadow of Gold.” Visit Thajiwas Glacier, where you can enjoy sledging and snow activities (depending on the season). After sightseeing, return to Srinagar for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day7

Srinagar - Doodhpathri

Take a day trip to Doodhpathri, an offbeat paradise with lush green meadows, river streams, and pine forests. Enjoy a peaceful day in nature before returning to Srinagar for the night.

Day8

Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your departure, marking the end of an unforgettable Kashmir tour.
